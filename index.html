What we do

Building 21st-century organisations

FutureGov is a change agency, on a mission to build 21st-century health and public sector organisations that are catalysts for change in the internet and climate era. We support organisations with digital transformation, service design and community development, building on the best of places to organise for change and radically improve outcomes for communities in the 21st century.

Ameo recently joined FutureGov, bringing with them a track record and expertise in local government restructuring and change management. Read more about Ameo.
